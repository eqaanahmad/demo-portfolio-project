How I Got Started in Ecommerce

During the COVID lockdown, when everyone was stuck at home, I started thinking, if people can’t go out, why not bring good design to them? That’s when I launched my first e-commerce store. I began selling home décor like frames, canvases, and neon signs, delivering them straight to people’s doors. Courier services were barely operating, so I packed and delivered most of the orders myself, sometimes in my own car, just to make it work. What started as a small hustle turned into a real business. And that’s where my journey began.
